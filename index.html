You can put markers to define waypoints in between and then click the 'Find best route' button
to get the best route from origin to the destination via these waypoints. You can also click to hold and then drag to alter the route.
Once you're done, press 'Place rider on this Route' button to place a moving marker on the route.
Once you're done with that, you can create another route and place another moving marker on it in a similar fashion.
